We are Trojans one and all...

Update (3/22/2016): Today marks the first day of spring practice for the Trojans and starts the second season for Troy's head coach Neal Brown. The Trojans improved by a game in the win loss column last season under Brown, the first season since 1990 without Legendary Troy head coach Larry Blakeney. The Trojans will hit the field every Tuesday, Thursday and Saturday of the Spring with spring ending with the annual T-Day spring game at 12:30 C.T.on April 23, 2016 on Larry Blakeney Field at Veterans Memorial Stadium. The Trojans will also have two other scrimages during the spring April 9, which will coninside with the Troy Sports Hall-of-Fame Banquet and Friday Night April 15, 2016 under the lights.

Update (3/3/2016): Today the Sun Belt conference released the 2016 conference schedule for the Trojans to complete the schedule. In major news the Trojans have two natioanlly televised games on Espn U. One of these televised games is the Battle of the Belt between the Trojans and in-state rival South Alabama on Thursday October 20, 2016 at South Alabama. The other is the end of a three game homestand and the final home of the 2016 schedule, which means its Senior Night for Troy as they play defending conference champs Arkansas State on November 17, 2016. For the first time in a couple years the Trojans open the season at home against FCS oppenent Austin Peay. Troy's head coach rumoured to retire after this season.

Update (2/3/2016): Today is National Signing Day 2016. That means its the day junior college and high school athletes sign a National Letter Intent to go to a school of there choice. The Trojans had one of the best recruiting classes in school history in Coach Neal Brown's second class. The class was so good to finished second in the Sun Belt Conference only trailing Georgia Southern, but it was good enough to finish in the top hundred. The Trojans signed three at a very depleted position of quarterback with only starting quarterback Brandon Silvers returning. But two of the three quarterback signees in Sawyer Smith and Ramsey Rigby enrolled in Janurary as Green Shirts.
